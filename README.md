# Game-Of-Life
Game of Life is a zero player game or a simulation.
It can be considered as a Cellular Automation where each cell has two states; alive or dead.

Being "Zero-Player" means its evolution is completely determined by its initial starting configuration, as such requires no manual interaction to it.

Cell either gets multiplied or diminished through: 

a. Underpopulation: A live cell with fewer than two neighbour dies,
b. Survival: A live cell with two or three neighbour lives on,
c. Overpopulation: A live cell with more than three dies,
d. Reproduction: A live cell with exactly three becomes alive. 

Also, mathematically known as Turing Complete.

Something simple yet helps recognize patterns and emergent systems
